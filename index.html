Gesture Library

☝️

Move Cursor

Extend your index finger. The cursor seamlessly follows your fingertip across all connected displays with predictive tracking.

🤏

Left Click

Quickly pinch your index and middle fingers together to perform a single left click.

✌️

Double Click

Rapidly pinch your index and middle fingers twice in quick succession to double click.

✊

Drag & Drop

Hold the pinch (index + middle) to enter drag mode. Move items across your screen effortlessly.

🤞

Right Click

Pinch your index and ring fingers together to trigger a contextual right click.

🖐

Scroll Up

Three fingers up to scroll up. Keep them extended to scroll continuously.

🤙

Scroll Down

Extend only your pinky finger to scroll down the page continuously.

🔊

Volume Control

Pinch your thumb and index fingers. Move your hand up and down to adjust the system audio.

💨

Switch Desktop

Keep all five fingers extended and perform a smooth horizontal swipe to switch macOS virtual desktops.

🌐

Mission Control

Keep all five fingers extended and perform a smooth vertical swipe up/down to open Mission Control or Exposé.

🔍

Zoom In/Out

Pinch your thumb and ring finger together. Move your hand up to zoom in, and down to zoom out.
